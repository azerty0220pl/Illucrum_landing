Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026 · Illucrum LLC

Illucrum does not use cookies, tracking scripts, or analytics on this website. We do not collect personal data through this site unless you contact us directly.

1. What We Collect

We collect only the information you provide when you contact us:

  • Your name and email address (if provided)
  • Your website URL (for audit purposes)
  • Any business information you share during our engagement
  • WhatsApp or Telegram messages sent to our business accounts

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to deliver the services you’ve requested: SEO audits, website optimisation, or web builds. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

3. Data Retention

We retain audit data and client communications for the duration of our engagement plus 12 months, after which data is deleted. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third-Party Services

Our website does not use third-party analytics, advertising pixels, or social media tracking. We use WhatsApp and Telegram as communication channels, these services have their own privacy policies. We may embed a Clutch.co review widget, which is subject to Clutch’s own privacy policy.

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at szymon.kokot@illucrum.com.

6. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at szymon.kokot@illucrum.com or via WhatsApp.
