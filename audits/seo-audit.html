What you get

Every audit produces a scored, professional report in PDF format. Your report includes:

  • A traffic-light severity score (green / amber / red) for every check
  • A numeric score (1–10) per section with an overall performance score
  • Plain-language explanations of what each finding means for your business
  • A prioritised action plan, what to fix first and why
  • A debrief session (30–45 min, via chat, call, or video) to walk through the findings

The Website SEO Audit is designed for small and mid-sized businesses that want a clear, honest assessment of where their site stands. You might be a good fit if:

  • You’ve never had a professional SEO audit before
