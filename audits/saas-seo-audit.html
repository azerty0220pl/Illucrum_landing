Why a generic audit isn’t enough for SaaS

Generic SEO audits treat every website the same way. They check your title tags, test your page speed, and tell you your meta descriptions are too long. That’s useful, but it misses the point for a software company.

SaaS SEO is fundamentally about funnel architecture. Your trial page needs to rank. Your pricing page needs to convert organic visitors. Your comparison pages, “[Your product] vs [Competitor]”, need to exist and need to rank. Your feature pages need to target the exact terms potential customers search at the consideration stage.

A generic audit has no concept of funnel-stage keyword mapping, trial page conversion signals, or programmatic SEO opportunities. This audit does.
