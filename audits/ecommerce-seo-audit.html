Why a generic audit won’t cut it

Your store has SEO problems that no generic audit will find

Online stores fail at SEO in ways that brochure sites and blogs never do. Shopify’s collection URL structure creates duplicate content at scale. WooCommerce tag pages bloat your crawl budget. Magento’s faceted navigation generates thousands of near-identical URLs. And on most stores we audit, Product schema is missing, malformed, or only half-implemented.

A generic audit grades you on fundamentals it was designed for. This audit was built from the ground up for online stores, with checks that map directly to how Google indexes and ranks product, collection, and category pages. Considering organic search drives a meaningful share of e-commerce revenue, the difference is real money.

Supported platforms: Shopify, Shopify Plus, WooCommerce, Magento, BigCommerce, Squarespace Commerce, and custom-built stores.

What the Audit Covers

7 areas, 46 individual checks. Where a generic audit grades you on fundamentals, this one digs into the structures that drive (or lose) organic revenue: product pages, collection architecture, schema, faceted navigation, and conversion friction.

1

Technical SEO

Page speed and Core Web Vitals on key templates, HTTPS, mobile rendering, sitemap, canonical tags, URL structure, schema markup, pagination, tag-page handling, redirects, crawl budget. 10 Essential + 2 Full.

2

On-Page SEO

Title tags, meta descriptions, heading structure, image alt text, internal linking, category page content depth, Open Graph. 6 Essential + 2 Full.

3

Product & Catalogue SEO

Product page quality, descriptions, collection architecture, variant handling, out-of-stock strategy, faceted navigation, product images, review schema. 5 Essential + 3 Full.

4

E-Commerce Keyword Strategy

Current rankings, product and category keyword coverage, long-tail opportunities, quick wins, keyword gaps, branded vs non-branded split. 4 Essential + 2 Full.

UX & Conversion

Competitor & SERP Analysis Full only

7

Backlink Profile Full only

Total referring domains, growth trend, anchor text distribution, toxic link detection, competitor link gap. 3 Full checks.
