Why e-commerce is different

Built for Online Stores, Not Generic Websites

E-commerce sites have SEO problems that no generic audit framework can find. Shopify’s collection URL structure creates duplicate content at scale. WooCommerce tag pages bloat your crawl budget. Magento’s faceted navigation generates thousands of near-identical URLs. Product schema is missing or malformed on most stores we audit.

A generic audit grades you on fundamentals it was designed for. Our e-commerce SEO audit was built from the ground up for online stores, with checks that map directly to how Google indexes and ranks product and category pages.

Supported platforms: Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento, BigCommerce, Squarespace Commerce, and custom-built stores.

What the Audit Covers

1

Technical SEO

Page speed, Core Web Vitals, HTTPS, mobile, sitemap, canonical tags, URL structure, schema markup, pagination, tag page handling, redirects, and crawl budget. 10 Starter + 2 Growth checks.

2

On-Page SEO

Title tags, meta descriptions, headings, images & alt text, internal linking, category content quality, content depth, and Open Graph tags. 6 Starter + 2 Growth checks.

3

Product & Catalogue SEO

Product page quality, descriptions, collection architecture, variant handling, out-of-stock strategy, faceted navigation, product images, and review schema. 5 Starter + 3 Growth checks.

4

E-Commerce Keyword Strategy

Current rankings, product and category keyword coverage, long-tail opportunities, quick wins, keyword gaps, and branded vs non-branded split. 4 Starter + 2 Growth checks.

5

Competitor & SERP Analysis (Full only)

Top 3 organic competitors, domain authority comparison, Shopping carousel presence, and content gap analysis. 2 Starter + 2 Growth checks.

6

Backlink Profile (Full only)

Total backlinks, toxic link detection, and competitor link gap analysis. 3 Growth checks.

7

UX & Conversion

Navigation, cart & checkout SEO impact, trust signals, page layout, and conversion funnel assessment. 4 Starter + 1 Growth check.
