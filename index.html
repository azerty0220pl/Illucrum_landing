Common questions

A professional SEO audit covers technical health (page speed, crawlability, HTTPS, mobile), on-page elements (title tags, meta descriptions, headings, content quality), keyword strategy, backlink profile, competitor analysis, and user experience. Illucrum’s audits use a 46-point structured checklist with severity scoring and a prioritised action plan.

Essential audits are delivered in 3–5 business days. Full audits, which include additional sections like competitor deep-dives and specialist analysis, take 5–8 business days. Each report includes a debrief session.
