Website SEO Audit vs SaaS SEO Audit

The Website SEO Audit is a broad-scope 46-point analysis suitable for any business type. The SaaS SEO Audit replaces generic sections with funnel-stage keyword analysis, trial page evaluation, comparison page strategy, and marketplace profile review. Choose the SaaS audit if your revenue depends on online signups, trials, or demos.
