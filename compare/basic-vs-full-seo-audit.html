Basic vs Full SEO Audit

A Basic SEO audit covers the core checks, technical SEO, on-page elements, keyword strategy, and UX; with a top-priorities action plan. A Full audit adds backlink analysis, competitor deep-dives, and complete specialist sections. The Basic is a strong standalone diagnostic. The Full gives you the complete picture and a comprehensive roadmap.

Which tier should you choose?

Choose Basic if...

This is your first SEO audit and you want a diagnostic starting point. You have a tight budget and want to validate before committing. You want the core findings fast. You plan to upgrade to Full later (Basic fee is credited toward Full).

Choose Full if...

You’re actively investing in growth and want the complete picture. You want competitor analysis: who’s outranking you and why. You need backlink insights. You want the full specialist sections and a comprehensive roadmap.
