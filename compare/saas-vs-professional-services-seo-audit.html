SaaS SEO Audit vs Professional Services SEO Audit

The SaaS audit focuses on acquisition funnel architecture: trial pages, comparison pages, funnel-stage keywords. The Professional Services audit focuses on E-E-A-T and trust signals: credentials, author authority, YMYL content quality. If you run a software company that serves regulated markets (legaltech, fintech, healthtech), you may need elements of both, contact us to discuss.

If you run a SaaS company in a regulated space, legaltech, fintech, healthtech; you may face both funnel SEO challenges and YMYL content scrutiny.
