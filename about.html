Meet Szymon Kokot

I’m a web developer and SEO consultant, originally from Poland, with a computer science background from the Universidad Politécnica de Madrid. After graduating, I moved straight into industry to build the kind of websites I cared about, rather than only study them.

I spent three years inside large B2B e-commerce companies in Spain, developing, maintaining, and SEO-optimising their platforms. That experience taught me how enterprise sites actually work: what breaks rankings, what accelerates them, and why most SEO advice falls apart the moment it meets a real codebase.

I started Illucrum because the same pattern kept repeating: small and mid-sized businesses getting either overcharged by agencies or underserved by automated tools. There’s a gap between a $50 robot report and a $5,000 agency engagement, and that’s where we work. Every audit is hands-on, specific to your business, and written to be actioned, not filed.

How we work

ROI first, always.

Every recommendation ties back to a business outcome. We prioritise findings by revenue impact, not score points or vanity metrics.

E-commerce native.

Our primary audit is built for online stores. Three years inside large e-commerce platforms means we catch the structural issues generic audits miss.

Reports are written for business owners. You won’t need a translator to understand your own audit, and you won’t be patronised either.
