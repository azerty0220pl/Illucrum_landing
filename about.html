Meet Szymon Kokot

I’m a web developer and SEO consultant, originally from Poland. I studied computer science at the Universidad Politécnica de Madrid before realising I’d learn more building things than sitting in lectures. So I did.

I spent three years working with large B2B e-commerce companies in Spain; developing, maintaining, and SEO-optimising their platforms. That experience taught me how enterprise sites work under the hood: what breaks rankings, what accelerates them, and why most SEO advice falls apart when it hits a real codebase.

I started Illucrum because I saw the same problems repeated everywhere: small and mid-sized businesses getting either overcharged by agencies or underserved by automated tools. There’s a gap between a €50 robot report and a €5,000 agency engagement. And that’s exactly where we operate. Every audit we deliver is hands-on, specific to your business, and designed to lead somewhere useful.
